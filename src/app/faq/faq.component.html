Smartwhite shirt HOW DO I CARE FOR MY SHIRT? Smart White shirts are long-wearing and easy to care for. All you need is to follow the instructions on the care label (Gentle machine or hand wash in cold water only). We recommend you turn all shirts inside-out during laundering. This both protects the buttons from chipping on the steel drum and allows the cleaning agents direct access to sweat stains. And a warm reminder, hang or lay shirt flat to dry to avoid shrinkage from tumble drying. To prolong the life of your shirt, avoid excessive heat and using little to no starch.
